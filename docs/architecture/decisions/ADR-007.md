## Decision
The frontend-to-backend question routing architecture shall be organized strictly through 24 fixed astrological master domains (12 core Bhava domains and 12 cross-domain areas).

## Architectural Rationale
This hierarchical blueprint provides a definitive skeleton that governs how frontend questions map to backend logic, ensuring infinite predictability when scaling from 24 categories to hundreds of specific queries.

## Consequences
All new UI questions shall map cleanly underneath one of the 24 Master Nodes in the Question Registry.
