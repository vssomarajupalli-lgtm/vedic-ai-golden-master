## Decision
The LLM acts purely as a formatter and shall be strictly forbidden from executing, inferring, or altering astrological mathematical logic.

## Architectural Rationale
This boundary protects the deterministic integrity of the Golden Master. If an LLM is permitted to execute logic or decide which astrological factors are relevant, the system inherently loses determinism and mathematical stability.

## Consequences
The generative AI layer shall receive completely pre-calculated, deterministic data payloads and shall only perform natural language translation and synthesis.
