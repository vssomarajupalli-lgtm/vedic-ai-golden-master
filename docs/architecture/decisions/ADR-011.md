# ADR-011: Exclusion of the Moon from the Biological Progeny Promise

## ADR ID
ADR-011

## Title
Exclusion of the Moon from the Biological Progeny Promise

## Status
Accepted

## Recovery Classification
Recovered during GM-005 Repository Refinement
BKL-005 ADR Recovery

## Context
During Phase 14 Hardening, specific choices were made regarding astrological domain modeling, particularly concerning the physical promise of children versus emotional desires. The role of the Moon was reviewed in this context.

## Repository Evidence
"The Moon rules the mind and emotions. While it plays a role in female fertility, the primary Parashari indicators for the creation of offspring are the 5th house and Jupiter. Adding the Moon dilutes the physical promise of a child with emotional desire..."

## Decision
The Moon shall not be used as a primary structural factor in calculating the physical promise of biological progeny.

## Architectural Rationale
This documents a highly specific, hard-won domain modeling choice that future astrologers might accidentally revert. Adding the Moon dilutes the physical promise of a child with emotional desire.

## Consequences
Formula models related to biological progeny will exclude the Moon as a primary indicator, maintaining the focus on the 5th house and Jupiter.
