# ADR-008: Evaluate Once, Consume Many (Single Source of Truth)

## Context
In Phase 12, there was a significant architectural risk that disparate systems (UI, LLM, PDF generators) might attempt to recalculate astrological facts independently, leading to fragmented and contradictory logic.

## Repository Evidence
"Detailed the 'Evaluate Once, Consume Many' dependency mapping for the computational engines... Downstream systems are strictly forbidden from recalculating astrology."

## Decision
The Formula Evaluator shall act as the solitary executor of astrological mathematical logic. All downstream systems shall exclusively consume its exact boolean payload.

## Architectural Rationale
This pattern establishes a Single Source of Truth, protecting against fragmented logic where the UI calculates one output and the backend calculates another. It mathematically locks the state of the chart prior to rendering.

## Consequences
UI dashboards, LLM Answer Composers, and PDF reports shall be purely "dumb consumers" of the evaluation payload. They shall not possess calculation logic.
