## Context

## Decision
The system shall explicitly forbid flattening or mathematically aggregating Natal Promise (static potential), Dasha (timing activation), and Transit (current sky) values into one unified score.

## Architectural Rationale
This restriction preserves the fundamental structural limits of the Vedic-AI architecture, ensuring that layered temporal analysis is maintained rather than simplified into arbitrary numerical sums that lose astrological meaning.

## Consequences
Mathematical engines shall remain isolated in their scoring. The Formula Evaluator shall evaluate these layers as distinct logical gates rather than cumulative variables.
