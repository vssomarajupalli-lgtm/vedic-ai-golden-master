# QUALITY METRICS ENGINE KNOWLEDGE PACKAGE

## 1. Executive Summary
The `QualityMetricsEngine` is a deterministic prediction quality measurement framework. It evaluates the mathematical health, internal coherence, and statistical distribution of the output generated by the astrological engines, ensuring the system produces explainable and well-calibrated results.

## 2. Purpose
To prevent the Golden Master from generating mathematically skewed or logically contradictory predictions. It acts as an automated QA layer for the mathematical output, enforcing that weights sum to 1.0, scores utilize a healthy standard deviation spread, and grades match their numeric thresholds.

## 3. Responsibilities
- **Score Distribution Analysis:** Calculate mean, median, std-dev, and histograms of domain/planet/house scores.
- **Sensitivity Analysis:** Determine the exact contribution percentage of each factor to the final Master Score.
- **Calibration Report:** Validate mathematical invariants (e.g., scores in range `[0, 100]`, weights sum to `1.0`).
- **Prediction Quality Scores:** Calculate Discrimination (ability to differentiate), Resolution (usage of range), and Coherence (internal consistency).

## 4. Architecture
- **Stateless Engine:** Zero AI/ML. Pure arithmetic analysis.
- Consumes the final `pipeline_output` dictionary. It does not calculate astrology; it calculates statistics *on* the astrology.

## 5. Inputs
- `pipeline_output` (dict): The complete output from `PipelineRunner.process()`.

## 6. Outputs
- `dict`: A structured quality report containing `distribution`, `sensitivity`, `calibration`, `quality_scores`, and a human-readable `summary`.

## 7. Data Contracts
- Validates that engine payloads conform to expected shapes and boundaries (e.g., ensuring `natal_promise` contains exactly 8 domains).

## 8. Formula Dependencies
- Standard deviation and mean algorithms.
- `discrimination_score`: std / 50.0 (normalized to 1.0).
- `resolution_score`: range_used / 100.0.
- `coherence_score`: 1.0 minus 0.25 per failed calibration check.

## 9. Engine Dependencies
- **Upstream:** Consumes the outputs of *all* other engines after they have been processed by the `PipelineRunner`.
- **Downstream:** Outputs are consumed by the Validation Framework (Frontend) and developer analytics.

## 10. Business Rules
- **Ideal Mean:** Must be between 35.0 and 70.0.
- **Ideal Std-Dev:** Must be between 10.0 and 35.0 (prevents scores from being too clustered or too polarized).

## 11. Deterministic Rules
- Performs exact float matching (with an epsilon of `1e-6` for weight sums).
- Uses strict grade-band logic to verify that `final_score` matches `grade`.

## 12. Execution Flow
1. Extracts `natal_scores`, `planet_scores`, and `house_scores` from the pipeline payload.
2. Computes `_score_distribution`.
3. Computes `_sensitivity_analysis` (Factor score * weight).
4. Computes `_calibration_check` (8 strict invariants).
5. Computes `_prediction_quality` (Discrimination, Resolution, Coherence).
6. Generates human-readable summary strings based on deviations.
