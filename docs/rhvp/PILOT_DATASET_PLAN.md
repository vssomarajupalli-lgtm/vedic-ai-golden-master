# RHVP Pilot Dataset Selection Plan

## Objective
Select a balanced sample of 10 to 20 highly verified, real-world horoscopes to test the deterministic behavior of the Golden Master Version 1.0 engine and establish the baseline effectiveness of the RHVP validation methodology itself.

## Selection Strategy (First 10 Cases)
To ensure broad coverage across different formula branches (Marriage, Wealth, Career, Health, etc.), the initial 10 cases must be purposefully curated:

1. **CASE-001 (Strong Success Chart):** Native with verified early, immense success. Tests positive Raja Yogas and strong planetary dignities.
2. **CASE-002 (Financial Difficulty):** Native with a verified history of bankruptcy or severe financial constraint. Tests wealth deprivation formulas (e.g., Daridra Yoga, weak 2nd/11th lords).
3. **CASE-003 (Career Growth - Salaried):** Native with a highly structured corporate/government career. Tests stable 10th house and Saturn/Sun dynamics.
4. **CASE-004 (Career Growth - Business):** Native who is a successful entrepreneur/business owner. Tests 7th/10th house business logic and Mercury/Mars influences.
5. **CASE-005 (Marriage - Early/Stable):** Native with a highly successful, stable marriage. Tests strong 7th house and Venus/Jupiter.
6. **CASE-006 (Marriage - Delayed/Divorce):** Native with verified marital strife, delay, or divorce. Tests 7th house afflictions, Mangal Dosha, and Saturn/Rahu impact.
7. **CASE-007 (Health-Focused):** Native with a verified history of a major chronic illness or surgery. Tests 6th/8th house activation and malefic transits.
8. **CASE-008 (Spiritual Life):** Native with verified intense spiritual dedication, renunciation, or guru diksha. Tests 9th/12th house and Ketu/Jupiter.
9. **CASE-009 (Male - General Mix):** A standard chart with mixed outcomes to test baseline noise.
10. **CASE-010 (Female - General Mix):** A standard chart with mixed outcomes, ensuring gender balance and diverse age representation.

## Next Steps (Cases 11-20)
Once the first 10 cases are processed, the remaining 10 will be dynamically selected to target any domains that showed high `VB` (Validation Backlog) frequency in the first batch, prioritizing stress-testing of specific mathematical boundaries.

*Reminder: Do not invent or simulate any horoscope data. All cases must be strictly backed by real-world, verified individuals.*
