## Status Definitions
- **Open:** Newly recorded Level 1 observation.
- **Monitor:** Seeking corroborating cases.
- **Pattern:** Level 2 - Recurring behavior identified across multiple cases.
- **Candidate:** Level 3 - Sufficient engineering evidence exists. Ready for future calibration phase.
- **Closed:** Observation rejected or resolved.
